How It Works

Add up your monthly income, and from there add up all the things you spend your money on. From reacurring bills, to weekly expenses. Put a savings goal, and if you go over your limit, we will help point out where you could maybe cut back and get to your goal. If you save more than you wanted at the end of the month, you can either keep it in your savings, or perhaps reward your self with a nice dinner. Its all up to you!

No More Empty Wallets

At the end of each month, you'll know exactly you have left over. No more empty wallets, no more looking surprised at the end of each month wondering where all your money went, not a penny missing!

Track Your Spending

Everyones chart will look different depending on his/her spending habits. Make a goal, set it in motion and we'll help you track it and do the rest!
